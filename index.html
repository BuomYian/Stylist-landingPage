Fashion Stylist

A web app for discovering and purchasing fashionable items and accessories.

Explore

Search feature

The users can search the product by department(category), price, avegrage customer review

Product page features

Here, the customer can either add the product to cart if they want to buy it or view it virtually. It is also in the page that a registered user can review the product

Admin Dashboard

In this page, the admin can know the number of users in the web app, the total orders that has been made by the customers and the total sales

About

The inspiration for my project came from my friends, Hermella and Haniyam, who are both models. They often shared their experiences and challenges when it came to finding the right fashion items and accessories for their shoots. This sparked the idea of creating a web application that would simplify the process of discovering and purchasing fashion items.
